Figure 8. This plot shows an interactive heatmap of various job types and their relationship to different schedule types.

It is very cleary from this heatmap that the majority of job titles, despite whether or not the job is associated with data analysis, data science or machine learning, are looking for full-time applicants. While this can be seen as a positive for DSAN graduates looking for full time jobs, students looking for internships might need to look a little further.
